Quarterly Business Summary

Executive Summary
The automation team delivered three high-impact internal tools this quarter: a document Q&A assistant, a reporting workflow monitor, and a spreadsheet reconciliation helper. The document Q&A assistant reduced manual lookup time for policy questions and improved consistency in answers.

Operational Highlights
The team processed 1,200 support knowledge-base articles and created a searchable retrieval layer for internal users. Average response time improved because employees could ask natural-language questions instead of manually browsing folders.

Risks and Constraints
The largest constraints are API rate limits, inconsistent document formatting, and missing source ownership. The team recommends local fallback models for development, page-aware citations for review workflows, and a monthly content refresh process.

Next Quarter Priorities
Next quarter will focus on evaluation datasets, automated quality checks, better PDF page references, and deployment hardening. The team also plans to add role-based access controls before expanding usage to additional departments.
